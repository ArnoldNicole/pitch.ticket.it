The Problem

Manual Ticketing & Revenue Leaks

Event organizers and football clubs in Kenya rely on outdated paper tickets and cash collections, leading to significant revenue losses and counterfeit tickets.

Missing Data & Analytics

Lack of data and analytics on existing ticketing platforms makes it hard for event organizers to understand their audience, track sales performance, or improve future events. Without clear insights, decisions are based on guesswork rather than real trends, leading to missed opportunities for growth and optimization. TicketIt addresses this gap by offering actionable analytics and real-time reporting.

Fragmented platforms and poor digital coordination

Fragmented platforms and poor digital coordination lead to inefficiencies, duplicated efforts, and a disjointed user experience. When systems don't communicate seamlessly, users are forced to navigate multiple channels, often re-entering the same information or facing delays due to lack of real-time data sharing. This not only reduces productivity but also hampers decision-making and service delivery.

No Standardized Access Control

Lack of reliable check-in systems leads to long queues, frustrated attendees, and overwhelmed event staff.
